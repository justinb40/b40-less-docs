table-bordered helper

Apply borders around all table cells.

Usage

<table class="table table-bordered">
    <thead>
        <tr>
            <th>Table Cell</th>
            <th>Table Cell</th>
            <th>Table Cell</th>
        </tr>
    </thead>
    <tbody>
        <tr>
            <td>Table Cell</td>
            <td>Table Cell</td>
            <td>Table Cell</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td>Table Cell</td>
            <td>Table Cell</td>
            <td>Table Cell</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td>Table Cell</td>
            <td>Table Cell</td>
            <td>Table Cell</td>
        </tr>
    </tbody>
<table>
