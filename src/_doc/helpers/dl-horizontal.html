dl-horizontal helper

Make terms and descriptions in definition lists line up side-by-side.

Terms that are too long to fit in the left column will be truncated with an ellipsis ...

Usage

<dl class="dl-horizontal">
    <dt>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</dt>
    <dd>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.</dd>
    
    <dt>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</dt>
    <dd>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.</dd>
    
    <dt>Lorem ipsum</dt>
    <dd>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.</dd>
</dl>
