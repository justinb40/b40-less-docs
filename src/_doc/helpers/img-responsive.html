img-responsive helper

Keep an image within the boundaries of its container. The image will automatically scale down when it is wider than its container.

This is often more helpful when used as a mixin.

Usage

<div class="row">
    <div class="grid-2">
        <img src="http://dummyimage.com/320x240/333/fff" class="img-responsive">
    </div>
</div>
