form-switch-inline helper

Use this helper to put checkboxes and radio buttons side-by-side on one line.

Usage

<div class="form-group">
    <label class="form-switch-inline">
        <input type="checkbox" value="1"> Checkbox 1
    </label>
    <label class="form-switch-inline">
        <input type="checkbox" value="2"> Checkbox 2
    </label>
    <label class="form-switch-inline">
        <input type="checkbox" value="3"> Checkbox 3
    </label>
<div>
